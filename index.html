What is EPP Lessons?

Welcome to EPP Lessons!😊 This is a simple website that we created with some of the Lessons you might be interested in. This contains a list of English topics for you to study. EPP(English Proficiency Program) subject teaches people not only how to learn and use English, but also how to expand their vocabulary and communicate effectively in English, removing language obstacles and allowing for open and intelligible communication.

This was created using a variety of sources to compile learnings and expertise about English Grammar. Our goal is to use this basic website to share and inform anybody about the english lessons. We hope that by creating this website, learners will be able to improve their English proficiency and further their study.

We've compiled a list of 15 English lessons that we'd like to share. It gives us great joy to build such a website, and we are delighted to share it with everyone. Each topic features a quiz to assist the learner understand what they've learned.

Thank you for visiting our website, and have fun browsing it!😉😍
